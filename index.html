What to Avoid for Solid Health

To achieve a rock-solid health status, what you remove from your diet is just as important as what you add. Limit or eliminate these from your daily routine.

Added & Refined Sugars

Found in sodas, candies, and baked goods. Spikes insulin, causes energy crashes, and feeds inflammation and weight gain.

Trans Fats & Seed Oils

Deep-fried foods and highly processed vegetable oils heavily contribute to heart disease and cellular inflammation.

Ultra-Processed Meats

Hot dogs, sausages, and deli meats often contain nitrates and preservatives linked to various long-term health complications.

Prolonged Inactivity

Sitting for 8+ hours a day slows metabolism and weakens muscles. Remember to stand up and walk around every hour.
